Sample bootlogos are included for each platform.

For TrimUI Smart, bootlogo must be 16bpp R5 G6 B5 bitmap, resolution 320x240 and less than 512KiB, rotated 90 degrees anticlockwise (240x320) and named bootlogo_tg2040.bmp
For TrimUI Brick/Smart Pro, bootlogo must be 32-bit bitmap, resolution not exceeding 1024x768 or 1280x720 depending on your device. Bootlogo must be named bootlogo_tg3040.bmp for Brick or bootlogo_tg5040.bmp for Smart Pro.
